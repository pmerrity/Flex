Flex

The coolest grid on the block

Simple

So I did a thing. This is a simple 24-column grid with precise values (no gutters).

The grid has 5 classes:

  • .row - Basically a clearfix wrapper that keeps floated elements from breaking your layout.

  • .col-# - Replace '#' with any number (1-24) or with 4p8 (for 20% because math).

  • .no-float - All columns (except 24) are floated left. This removes the float. Best used with .auto-margin for centering.

  • .auto-margin - Basically a clearfix wrapper that keeps floated elements from breaking your layout.

Responsible

This grid is so responsive yo mama will put you up for adoption because this grid can responsibly handle itself more efficiently.

Not funny?

Yeah I know. That's why my ex left me (true story).

But srsly. Sincerest apologies to your dear mother. Keep reading:

  • The grid breaks at 4 of the most common breakpoints (480px, 600px, 768px, and 992px).

  • I did my best to break the columns using the best web standards. Examples:

    • .col-12 (50%) becomes 100% for tablet and below.

    • .col-6 (25%) becomes 50% for tablet and 100% on mobile.

    • .col-2 (8.33%) and .col-22 (91.66%) fit next to each other (like a logo and navbar). They break to 50% around tablet and then break to 100% for mobile.

  • IMPORTANT NOTE: There is one weird column class (.col-4p8) that equals 20% (or 1/5).

    • The reason it's a strange name is because 4 divided by 24 is 16.66% and 5 divided by 24 is 20.833%.

    • Rounding would've broken everything and required gutters n stuff.

    • If you can think of a better name let me know smarty pants.
